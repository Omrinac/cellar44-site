Subterranean Craft

Beneath the bustling city streets, our team operates in a realm of shadow and precision. Here, the slow maturation of fine wine meets the immediate intensity of the flame.

EXECUTIVE CHEF

Julian Vane

With over two decades spent in the most prestigious kitchens across Europe, Chef Vane brings a philosophy of ‘Elemental Refinement’ to Cellar 44. His approach centers on the transformation of seasonal ingredients through forgotten subterranean techniques—curing, aging, and slow-fire roasting.
