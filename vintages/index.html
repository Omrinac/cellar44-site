THE WINE LIST

Curated Vintages & Rare Finds

Over 300 labels selected by Elena Marchetti — from prestigious Burgundies and structured Barolos to emerging natural wines and forgotten appellations.

BURGUNDY

Pinot Noir & Chardonnay

The heartland of our list. From village wines to premier crus, we stock over 60 Burgundian labels spanning three decades of vintages.

FEATURED LABELS

Domaine Leflaive, Méo-Camuzet, Rossignol-Trapet

RHÔNE VALLEY

Syrah & Grenache Blends

Bold, spiced, and structured — the Northern Rhône offers some of the most age-worthy reds in France. The Southern Rhône provides warmth and generosity.

FEATURED LABELS

Château Rayas, E. Guigal, Domaine du Vieux Télégraphe

NATURAL WINES

Low-Intervention Producers

Our growing natural selection favours producers who farm organically or biodynamically and intervene as little as possible in the cellar.

FEATURED LABELS

Cornelissen, Gut Oggau, COS

ITALY

Barolo, Brunello & Beyond

From the structured heights of Barolo to the coastal lightness of Vermentino, Italy’s diversity is represented across 50+ labels.

FEATURED LABELS

Bruno Giacosa, Gaja, Montevertine
