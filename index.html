THE HERITAGE

Subterranean Elegance

For eighty years, Cellar 44 has remained the city’s most cherished secret. Originally a private vault for the aristocracy’s finest vintages, it evolved into a sanctuary for those who seek the refined pleasure of silence, stone, and grape.

Our walls tell a story of decadence and restraint. Every brick, every rack, and every bottle has been curated to provide an escape from the frantic pace of the world above. Here, time is measured not by hours, but by the gradual opening of a rare Cabernet.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Affordable Sophistication

We believe luxury shouldn’t be loud. Our cellar is built on the principle of ‘Memorable Moments’—providing a high-end sensory experience that remains accessible to those who truly appreciate the art of dining. It is not just about the meal; it’s about the atmosphere that lingers long after the last drop is gone.
